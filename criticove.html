Criticove

The project

As amateurs of literature, my teammates an I thought to create an app that might help us review and rate our favorite reads on the go. As that already exists on the market, we extended our idea to include other types of media like movies and TV shows.

Our main focus was allowing users to keep their reviews within an intimate circle, wether that be just them or a few select friends.

Research

Personas

We devised three user personas that represent our target audience to get a better understanding of what features

  • Eddy represents young adults that want to share their latest reviews and see what their friends may have enjoyed recently. He may also use a platform like ours for something like a book club where reviewing books is a reccuring event.

  • Meriem represents more introverted people that may not want to share anything but instead want to keep a record of what they have consummed recently. Seeing when they watched a certain movie and what they thought of it at the time may prove useful to such people.

  • Kevin represents busier people that still want a way to connect with friends and family deespite their schedule. They may not post a lot themselves, but still want to see what their loved ones enjoyed.

High-Fidelity Prototyping

Because we want to foster creativity and offer a space for users to reflect and share reviews with no judgement, we prioritized a cool color palette and rounded shapes. In addition, we picked a non-serif font that also has more rounded edges.

I originally picked a color scheme including shades of muted green and eggplant for contrast. But, after thinking about the image we wanted for our app, we decided against it. To keep with the theme of the "cove", we wanted to evoke water by choosing shades of blue with hints of yellow for the sun.

Development

Once we were satisfied with the first iteration of our design, we moved on to the development phase. During this phase, some minor changes were made as we iterated on the design further and received feedback from peers.
